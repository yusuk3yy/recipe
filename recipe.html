RECIPE OF PIZZA

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • pizza dough
  • salt & pepper

  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • olive oil
  • sugar
  • pepperoni

steps to prepare

  • slide the pizza off of the peel onto the baking stone
  • bake pizza in the 475*F oven, one at a time
  • wait until the crust is browned and the cheese is golden, about 10 to 15 minutes

Optional Toppings

  • you can add a cheese or a little of salt if the taste isn't enough

  • add a la pepper if you prefer spicy on your pizza
